Orange Smoothie

A healthy drink made with fresh oranges.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : orange smoothie healthy drinks

instructions

step 1

Combine all ingredients in a blender, blend until smooth.

step 2

Pour mix into glass. Enjoy!

ingredients

4 peeled oranges

2 cups ice

1/3 cup milk

2 tablespoons honey

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

tools

Blender
