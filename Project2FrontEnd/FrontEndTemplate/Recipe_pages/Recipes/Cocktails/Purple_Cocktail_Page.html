Purple Cocktail

A vibrant cocktail that tastes as good as it looks!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : purple cocktail alcohol drinks

instructions

step 1

Add liquids to shaker, shake well.

step 2

Pour into glass with ice. Garnish with lemon and enjoy!

ingredients

1 1/2 oz. vodka

3 oz. lemonade

1/2 oz. Blue Curacao

1/4 oz. Grenadine

Lemon slices for garnish

tools

Cocktail Shaker
